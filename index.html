About

Veto Capital is a berlin based investment collective, helping entrepreneurs to build their companies. It does so by adding money and knowledge in the very early stages.

Its members don't only provide the investment, but also experience in finance and legal matters, in setting up teams and scaling them, in product and project management and in selecting and incorporating the right technologies.

Veto Capital focuses on IT, Advertising and AdTech, but also invests in other industries.

Team

We offer both, money and people: smart, qualified, motivated business angels who are easy to collaborate and communicate with. Here’s a small look at who we are and what we have done so far.
